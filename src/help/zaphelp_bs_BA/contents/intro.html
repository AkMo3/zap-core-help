OWASP ZAP Korisničko Upustvo

Dobrodošli u OWASP Zed Attack Proxy (ZAP) Korisničko Upustvo.

Ono je dostupno kao kontekstno-osjetljiva pomoć unutar ZAP-a i online http://code.google.com/p/zaproxy/wiki/HelpIntro

ZAP je jednostavan alat za korištenje integriranih penetracijskih testiranje za pronalaženje ranjivosti u web aplikacijama.

It is designed to be used by people with a wide range of security experience and as such is ideal for developers and functional testers who are new to penetration testing.

ZAP provides automated scanners as well as a set of tools that allow you to find security vulnerabilities manually.

If you are new to ZAP then its recommended that you look at the Getting Started section.

ZAP is a fork of the open source variant of the Paros Proxy.
